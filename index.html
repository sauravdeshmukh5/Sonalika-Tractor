About Us

'Videshon ko Sabse Zyada Export Hone wala tractor brand’, Sonalika Tractors is currently the No.1 tractor exports brand from India. It is also the 3rd largest tractor manufacturer in the country as well as stands proudly among the top 5 tractor manufacturers around the world. Incorporated in 1996 with farmer centricity at its core DNA, the company manufactures customised tractors and implements which are developed as per region as well as application centric requirements of farmers.
